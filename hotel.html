Our Story

A Legacy of
Timeless Luxury

Since 1924, Aurelia Grand has set the benchmark for extraordinary hospitality. Perched above the Indian Ocean, our overwater sanctuary blends Maldivian tradition with contemporary elegance — a place where every detail is an act of devotion to your comfort.

Our 48 private villas float above the turquoise lagoon, each offering direct ocean access, a private infinity pool, and unobstructed views of the most spectacular sunsets on Earth.
